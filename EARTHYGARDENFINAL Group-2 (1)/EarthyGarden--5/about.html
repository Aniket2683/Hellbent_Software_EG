About Us

  • home /
  • about us

Welcome to EarthyGarden, where we, an elderly couple, have turned our lifelong love for flowers into a business. We have been married for over 50 years and have spent most of our lives tending to our garden and nurturing beautiful blooms. We have now decided to share our passion with the world and offer our expertise in the form of stunning floral arrangements.

Our journey started when we realized that there was a need for a local florist in our community. We noticed that people were constantly searching for fresh and beautiful flowers, but often struggled to find them. We saw this as an opportunity to provide our community with high-quality flowers that are locally grown and expertly arranged.

We take great pride in sourcing the freshest and most beautiful flowers from local farmers and carefully arranging them into stunning bouquets that are sure to brighten up any space. Our floral designs are created with love and attention to detail, and we are always willing to go the extra mile to ensure our customers are satisfied.

We believe that flowers have the power to bring joy and happiness to people's lives, and we are committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience. We are passionate about what we do and are always striving to improve and innovate.

Thank you for choosing our flower delivery service. We look forward to sharing the beauty and joy of flowers with you.
